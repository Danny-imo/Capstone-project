The Global Agricultural Summit serves as a crucial platform that unites a diverse community of experts, academics, and activists who form the creative commons affiliate network. With the aim of addressing pressing agricultural challenges, this influential event takes place in a different country every two years. In each edition, the summit brings together thought leaders and stakeholders from various backgrounds, fostering collaboration and knowledge exchange.
